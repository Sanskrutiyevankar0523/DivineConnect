Why this is auspicious — AI Explanation

This morning window is exceptionally auspicious for your Griha Pravesh. Here's why:

  • Abhijit Muhurat — This is considered universally auspicious across all Jyotish traditions. It occurs at midday and is ruled by Lord Vishnu, the preserver, making it ideal for new beginnings in a home.
  • Pushya Nakshatra — One of the three most favorable Nakshatras for Griha Pravesh. Pushya means "nourishment" and is associated with prosperity, growth, and protection of the home.
  • Shukla Dwitiya — The second day of the waxing moon phase symbolizes growth and new endeavors. Your home's energy will grow alongside the moon.
  • No Rahu Kaal — This window is completely clear of Rahu Kaal (10:30 AM - 12:00 PM), ensuring no malefic planetary influence during your ceremony.
